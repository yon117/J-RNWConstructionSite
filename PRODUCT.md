# Product

## Register

brand

> Admin panel (`/admin`, `/adminside`) is product register. Override per task when working on those surfaces.

## Users

Residential homeowners and commercial business owners evaluating a contractor. They arrive skeptical — they want proof of quality before making contact. Many visit on a phone, often in the field or between appointments. Secondary user: the J-RNW owner managing leads, projects, and services via the admin panel.

## Product Purpose

J-RNW Construction's public website and back-office tool. The public site builds trust through showcased work and makes it easy to get a quote. The admin panel manages incoming messages, services, and project portfolio without needing a developer.

## Brand Personality

Rugged, premium, approachable. The confidence of serious craftsmanship, the warmth of a local family business. Does exceptional work and lets it speak. Not a startup. Not a corporation. Not a flashy luxury brand.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS cream**: soft whites, Inter on beige, rounded everything — looks like a tech startup, not a contractor.
- **Low-budget contractor sites**: clip art hammers, stock hard hats, safety-yellow CTAs, Comic Sans energy.
- **Cold corporate**: all-blue enterprise palettes, zero warmth, feels like an insurance company.
- **Overdone luxury**: heavy gold fills, marble textures, yacht-club excess — the existing gold accent is earned, not decorative.

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't claim.** Portfolio quality and photo craft build trust faster than headlines.
2. **Warmth without softness.** Approachable enough to call, solid enough to trust with your building.
3. **Mobile is the first draft.** The client is on a phone. Every layout decision passes the one-thumb test first.
4. **Bilingual as native.** EN and ES are equal paths — copy, layout, and typography must hold up in both languages.
5. **Color as character.** The navy-and-gold palette stays; it's used with precision, not broadcast across every surface.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum (contrast, keyboard nav, screen reader support)
- Strong bilingual EN/ES — both languages are first-class experiences
- Mobile-first priority — exceptional phone UX before desktop enhancement
- Cross-device: iPhone (Safari/iOS WebKit), Android (Chrome), desktop Safari, Firefox — all must render correctly
- Better typography than current Inter default — improved readability in both languages
