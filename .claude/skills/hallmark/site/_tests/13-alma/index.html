02 / The two minutes

Speak. Alma writes it down. The session ends on its own.

  1. i

    Open Alma. Tap the dot. The dot pulses for two minutes, then stops. There is no countdown ring.

  2. ii

    Speak. Whatever's on your mind. The transcription happens on-device — the audio never leaves your phone.

  3. iii

    The dot stops. Alma saves the transcript and the audio to your private library. You can mark "keep" or do nothing — the default is forget.

After 365 days, Alma forgets what you didn't keep.
