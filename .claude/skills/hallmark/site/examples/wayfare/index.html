01 — What this is

Most travel sites ask you what you want and then sell you what they have. We do the opposite. Each Tuesday a board goes up. Six places. Six trips someone here has actually taken in the last twelve months.

You pick the row that calls. We do the rest — flights, the bed, the one restaurant that justifies the flight. No upsells, no loyalty tier, no plus members save. One flat planning fee, paid once you say go.
